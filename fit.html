Getting started

Is Anchor Neuro the right fit for you?

Finding the right provider matters as much as finding care at all. Here is an honest look at who we help, how we work, and how to take the first step, so you can feel confident before you reach out.

We may be a great fit if you are looking for

  • Thoughtful psychiatric care and medication management, with a provider who treats you as a whole person, not a symptom list.
  • A drug-free option, alongside or instead of medication, through EEG neurofeedback and Brainspotting.
  • Trauma-informed care that moves at your pace, including discreet, confidential support for service members, veterans, and their families.
  • Help with anxiety, depression, trauma and PTSD, ADHD, mood, OCD, grief, sleep, stress and burnout, or simply not feeling like yourself.
  • In-person care in Virginia Beach, or secure telehealth anywhere in Virginia.

If we're not the right fit, we'll help you find care that is

  • You are located outside Virginia. We can only see clients physically in Virginia, and we are glad to help point you toward care where you are.
  • You need urgent or emergency help right now. Please use the crisis resources below; we are not a crisis service.
  • You are looking only for ongoing controlled-substance prescriptions.[Confirm the exact wording and policy before launch]
  • [Confirm any other limits: ages seen, conditions referred out, etc.][To finalize before launch]
If you are in crisis or thinking about harming yourself, call or text 988 (Suicide and Crisis Lifeline; veterans press 1) or go to your nearest emergency room. If we are not the right fit, we will do our best to point you somewhere that is.

Insurance and self-pay

We accept insurance and self-pay, so cost is one less barrier to getting started. If your plan is not listed, we will share self-pay options before your consult, so there are no surprises.

  • In-network with Aetna, Anthem / HealthKeepers, BlueCross BlueShield, Carelon, Cigna / Evernorth, and Sentara
  • Self-pay welcome, with rates shared up front
  • In-person in Virginia Beach and telehealth across Virginia
