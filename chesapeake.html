Care that fits a Chesapeake life

Built for people who do not have a free afternoon to spare.

Chesapeake is one of the largest and fastest-growing communities in Hampton Roads, full of working parents, professionals, students, and families balancing more than they can usually fit in a day. When the calendar is already full, the hardest part of getting help is often just making room for it.

That is the problem Anchor Neuro is set up to solve. You do not have to choose between caring for yourself and keeping up with everything else. Most of your care can happen over secure video, on your schedule, without a commute or a waiting room. When in-person time is the right call, the office is a short drive away.

One honest note up front. Anchor Neuro does not have a Chesapeake office. We have one office, in Virginia Beach. We serve Chesapeake through secure telehealth anywhere in Virginia and through that nearby Virginia Beach office. No hidden locations, no marketing addresses.

A whole-person approach

Anchor Neuro is led by Courtney Davis, DNP, APRN, PMHNP-BC, BCN, a board-certified psychiatric nurse practitioner who is also board certified in neurofeedback. She earned her DNP from Old Dominion University, right here in Hampton Roads.

Care is integrative and trauma-informed, which means looking at the whole picture rather than a single symptom. That might mean medication, or therapy-style work, or brain-based approaches, or a thoughtful combination. The goal is a plan that actually fits your life in Chesapeake, not a one-size template.

Anchor Neuro also offers discreet care for service members, veterans, and their families, and is a member of The Headstrong Project. Courtney is the spouse of an Army veteran, and that understanding shows up in how she works with military households across Hampton Roads.

Questions from Chesapeake

Do you have an office in Chesapeake?

No. Anchor Neuro has one office, and it is in Virginia Beach at 1206 Laskin Rd, Suite 201-G, in the Hilltop area. We serve Chesapeake two ways. Secure telehealth is available anywhere in Virginia, so most of your care can happen from home or work with no commute. When you want or need to be seen in person, the Virginia Beach office is a short drive away. Neurofeedback is offered in the office only, so those sessions take place in Virginia Beach.

Can I do everything by telehealth from Chesapeake?

Most of it, yes. Integrative psychiatric care, medication management, and Brainspotting can be done over secure video from anywhere in Virginia. The one exception is EEG neurofeedback, which is offered in the office only. If neurofeedback becomes part of your plan, those visits happen at the Virginia Beach office.

Is telehealth a good fit for a busy schedule?

That is often why people choose it. Telehealth removes the drive and the waiting room, so a visit can fit into a lunch break, a quiet hour at home, or the window before the school pickup. You join from a private space on your phone or computer. For working parents and professionals juggling full days, that flexibility is frequently the difference between starting care and putting it off.

What insurance do you accept?

Anchor Neuro is in network with Aetna, Anthem and HealthKeepers, BlueCross BlueShield, Carelon, Cigna and Evernorth, and Sentara. Coverage and benefits vary by individual plan, so it is worth confirming yours before your first visit. The free consultation is a good moment to ask any insurance questions.

What happens at the free consultation?

It is a short, no-pressure conversation to see whether Anchor Neuro is a good fit. You can describe what is going on, ask about telehealth versus in-person care, talk through insurance, and get a feel for how Courtney works. There is no obligation to schedule anything afterward.

Does neurofeedback cure anxiety, PTSD, or trauma?

No, and anyone who promises a cure should give you pause. Neurofeedback is an emerging, promising approach that some people find helpful as part of a broader plan, and Brainspotting is a newer modality with a still-developing research base. Courtney will be honest about what each option can and cannot do for your situation, and care is tailored to you rather than sold as a guarantee.
